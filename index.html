Process

How I takle every projects

  • The Wish List

    Deffining Project Scope

    Defining client's vision of their brand and translate that into an online space

  • Branding

    Make things pretty

    Color, typography and setting the right ambiance of the website that reflects your brand

  • Interface Design

    Prototyping

    Content is king. Interface Design is the throne. A good interface design does not hinder the users from going to your webpage but instead, it should feel like roller skating to a part - smooth and fun

  • User Experience Design

    The great balancing act

    Wanna have a pretty website but still available to everyone? That's where User Experience design comes. The information architecture of your site, the number of clicks to buy something on your site and much more contribute to a good user experience. Optimizing your site for the comfort of your audience is the key to retain your audience

  • Coding

    The building blocks

    The goal is to make your website the way you wanted it to look and feel but are also dynamic. You want to make your site easy to understand and able to keep up with the changes within your business. Different Content Management System (such as WordPress) will be able to cater to your needs. Customized website using bootstrap is also a great idea to stand out.

  • Everyones Happy

    Project Completion

    Ticking all the checkbox and final adjustment as per client's request. Lunching the Website! Huray!!!

About

Ivan Apolonio

Passionate about Graphic Design and Web Development, always eager to tackle what every new challenges life throws at me. Experienced in making and maintaining websites for personal projects, volunteering and paid contracts using WordPress and by hand coding as well as creating an image banner, editing or taking photos, designing a UI and UX for any interactive media, I believe I can contribute to your project and give a fresh perspective for the betterment of your website. Experience in SEO, Information architecture and social media marketing through volunteering, and personal projects for small to medium size business. Always eager to learn more through free online courses to improve my skills in coding and reading online articles that delved into the latest SEO tactics.
